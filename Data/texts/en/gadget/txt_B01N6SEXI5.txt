The VisionTek Radeon 7750 SFF 2GB GDDR5 2x DP allows you to power the latest displays at the highest resolutions. With full support for 4K displays you can view even more content than ever. Independent resolutions allow you to have one 4K monitor and one high definition (1080p) or any combination of the two. A short form factor design allows you to add additional monitors to Short Form Factor, Mini ATX, and other small case systems. Bus powered design means you don’t have to worry about additional power pins. NOTE: This is not an HDMI graphics card. This graphics card has 2x DisplayPort outputs both of which support audio. If signal type translations are required for the desired configuration, active-type adapters are required. 3840x2160 resolution can be achieved on a 4K monitor while also using a cable that supports this resolution. SFF graphics cards refer to the height of the unit and bracket which allows for use in systems that are not full-height desktops. Two graphics cards can be used in tandem so long as the computer supports the operation of two simultaneously.
