The Lightning Audio + Charge RockStar makes it possible to listen to Lightning Audio while also acting as an iPhone charger. Enjoy music or talk through Lightning Audio headphones while you charge. Use this RockStar lightning cable anywhere; at home, at work, in the car or on the go. Works as an iPhone charger to any device that runs iOS 10 or later, including iPhone 14, iPhone 13, iPhone 12, and iPhone 11. This adapter acts as an iPad charger and is MFi-certified Lightning to USB cables & MFI-certified Lightning Audio headphones. Please note: The RockStar delivers power to your iPhone/iPad, and only supports Lightning Audio. It does not support syncing or any other USB data, or similar protocols like Serial or UART.
