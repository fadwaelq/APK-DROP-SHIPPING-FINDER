Tripp Lite's Power It! 4-Outlet Power Strip offers reliable, convenient, and cost-effective power distribution for appliances, power tools, lighting, and other electrical equipment. It includes 4 NEMA 5-15R outlets, a 6 ft. AC power cord, and keyhole mounting slots. The multi-outlet strip reduces cable clutter and enables the connection of multiple electrical devices to a single AC wall socket. It features a rugged metal case to stand up to the most demanding environments, an illuminated power switch with transparent cover to prevent accidental shutoff, and a 15 amp integrated circuit breaker to prevent dangerous overloads.
