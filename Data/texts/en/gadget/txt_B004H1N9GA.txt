Axion AXN-8706 widescreen portable LCD TV gives you the ability to enjoy your favorite television programs anywhere in your home - even away from home. Watch the morning news without having to get out of bed, or while sun bathing in the backyard. The LCD TV has a 7-Inch widescreen LCD that offers a high-resolution & high-brightness display. The built-in ATSC tuner and the detachable external antenna enable you to receive crisp clear reception of your favorite TV channels. Enjoy your favorite movie, music and photo files on the go with the built-in digital media player. The rechargeable Lithium-Polymer battery offers you to watch several hours of your favorite TV shows outdoor without any recharging needs.
