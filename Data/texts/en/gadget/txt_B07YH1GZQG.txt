Tripp Lite's U360-004-IND is a rugged, all metal, USB 3.0 SuperSpeed 4-Port hub is ideal for use in data centers, work areas and Industrial applications. Can be mounted on a wall or din rail with included mounting hardware. Supports SuperSpeed USB 3.0 transfer rates at 5 Gbps, but is also backward compatible to USB 2.0 and 1.1 (480mbps and 12mbps). Hub is bus-powered through the computer connection, or can self power with an external adapter (not included) or via the included 3-wire terminal block connector (9-40V DC).
