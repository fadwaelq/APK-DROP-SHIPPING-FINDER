If you hate any kind of light in the bedroom at night,you came to the right place. The led stickers will help you get away from  LED lights, it can dim overly bright lights on clocks, routers and various other devices.And say goodbye to ugly pieces of tape. PLEASE NOTE: 1. If you are looking for blackout stickers(completely block the light), you should buy 80%~100%.  If you want to dim light to where you need it,pls buy 50%~80%. 2. These light stickers has good adhesive on first placement, if you pull the stickers off and adjust them, they will lose some of adhesiveness. 3. It sticks well on room temperature hard plastic/metal/glass surfaces.But If you apply around a corner and not just on a flat surface,they kind of peel off on their own. 4. Please clean the surface of the item and sweat from your fingers before the stickers are applied, otherwise the stickiness will become poor.
