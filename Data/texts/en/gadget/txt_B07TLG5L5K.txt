HDi Audio Premium Portable AM/FM Radio features a large, easy-to-read radio dial with a separate scale for AM and FM tuning. It also has a large band indicator with a tuning LED indicator for low-light tuning. It's equipped with a ferrite rod AM antenna, a telescoping FM antenna, and 4" dynamic speaker for delivering crisp, high-quality sound. A 3.5mm port accepts mono earphones. This radio can be powered using the included AC cable or batteries, so you can use it at home or on the go.
