The BD-H5100 Blu-ray Disc Player from Samsung is designed for high-definition movie playback and streaming multimedia content. This Blu-ray player supports full HD 1080p Blu-ray disc playback from its HDMI output, and can also upconvert standard definition DVDs to near HD quality. This model features built-in streaming apps, which gives you access to a library of free and subscription-based Internet streaming content. An Ethernet port is built into the Blu-ray player for Internet connectivity. The HDMI output supports high-resolution audio formats such as Dolby TrueHD and DTS-HD for a robust multi-channel surround sound experience. The Blu-ray player's USB port allows you to connect devices such as thumb drives for easy playback of multimedia files. As an ENERGY STAR rated device, this Blu-ray player was designed to keep power consumption to a minimum
