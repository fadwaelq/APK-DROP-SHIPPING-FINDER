The Cirago DisplayPort to VGA Active Adapter allows the connection of a DisplayPort enabled computer to a VGA monitor or other VGA display. The Adapter has a built-in converter chip making the digital-to-analog signal conversion for VGA display. Connectors - Input: 20-pin DisplayPort Male - Output: 15-pin VGA Female Specifications - 800x600, 1024x768, 1280x768, 1366x768, 1280x1024, 1400x1050 1440x900, 1600x900, 1600x1200, 1680x1050, 1920x1080, 1920x1200@60HZ - HDTV resolution supported: 480i, 576i, 480p, 576p, 1080i and 1080p @60HZ - Bandwidth: Pixel rate up to 240MHz at 6bit per color / 180MHz at 8bit per color
