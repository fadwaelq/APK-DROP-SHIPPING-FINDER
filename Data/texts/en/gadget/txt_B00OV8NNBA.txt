Electriduct Cable Ties are made of general-purpose Nylon 6/6. The smooth, rounded edges ensure that your cables, wires, and your fingers are not cut, scraped nor damaged with use. Nylon cable ties are a self-locking, one piece tool, with a low-profile head that conserves space. These zip ties are used for securing loose wires, cables, and cords. Closely spaced positive locking teeth allow for fine adjustments when used to tie bundles of cables in a variety of shapes, sizes, and thicknesses. Head and pawl are integrally molded in this item for additional strength and durability. The variety of colors allows you to create a coding system, which can come in handy if using it for multiple objects. It locks tight and will not stretch nor slide, these cable ties are intended for permanent installation. V-shaped raised edges give it a sure grip and easy pull-up. Does not contain metal parts.
