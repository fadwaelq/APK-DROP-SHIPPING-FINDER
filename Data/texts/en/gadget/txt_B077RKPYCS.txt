QualGear qg-tm-032-blk is a UL listed heavy duty full motion wall mount is a mount that has the capacity of holding up to 40kg/88lbs that fits most 37"-70" Tvs. This mount places the TV just 3.03inches from the wall to complement the sleek look of ultra-thin TVs. It also extends up to 21.57inches from the wall, swivels and tilts perfect viewing angle from any seat in the room. With easy installation, you also get a foolproof locking bar that secures your TV and provides anti-theft protection (padlock not included). Add-On bubble level is included to check to see if the mount is perfectly level. Supports standard mounting hole patterns (VESA) up to 600x400.
