Astound trickor-treaters and partygoers with a gathering of ghouls and spirits who levitate, shriek and startle the mortals of this world! Released from human nightmares, the haunting specters of Phantasms are the anticipated follow-up to our best seller, Ghostly Apparitions. Displayable on a TV/monitor, wall, or window, this DVD features multiple display modes and a soundtrack with spooky music. Pnantasms is also optimized to create a hologram/illusion effect when projected onto mesh. Multiple effects can be played as a loop or triggered manually. Requires DVD player and a TV/monitor or projector.
