The LiDCAM plus is a high definition action camera that clips right to the bill of any cap. It is the hands-free solution for recording your own memories and outdoor experiences. The LiDCAM records exactly what your own eyes see, making it possible to relive and share your outdoor memories for years to come. Wi-fi connectivity turns your smart phone into a wireless remote, allowing you to start, stop, and view the recordings you've made while out in the field. With the LiDCAM, you'll be sure to get the shot, every time!
