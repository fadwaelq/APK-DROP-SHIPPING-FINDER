This pack of Color Instant Film from Impossible contains eight sheets of film for Polaroid 600 and Impossible I-type cameras, and features a 3.5 x 4.2" white frame with a glossy image area of 3.1 x 3.1". The film uses an innovative color protection formula that gives the photos rich, distinctive colors and balanced tones with a high level of detail and sharpness. Full development will occur approximately 30-40 minutes after exposure when working in a temperature of around 70°F / 21°C. + TheImagingWorld Micro Fiber Cloth.
