The Learning Steps for RM-991 1. Press the equipment key you want to learn, and it will switch to the appliance mode. ( For example, if you are learning from your TV remote, please press the TV key. ) 2. Press and hold the ‘set’ key until the ‘CODE’ is displayed, and then release. 3. Press the right key twice to enter the ‘LEARN’ menu. 4. Press the ‘OK’ key twice to enter the learning mode. 5. Press the key you want to learn on the original remote, and then press corresponding key on the RM-991. 6. After finishing, press the ‘SET’ key to exit learning mode. And press the ‘EXIT’ key to return to the TV interface. Other matching methods and function settings please read the user manual, it can help you!
