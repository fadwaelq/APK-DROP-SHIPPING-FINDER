The CyberPower 1 Outet Essential Surge Protector Wall Tap offers vital surge protection for common homes and office devices such as computers and electronics. It prevents spikes in energy by storms and electrical power surges from reaching your electronic equipment. The CSB100W provides 900 Joules of protection, a surge protected outlet (widely spaced for transformer plugs) and a wall tap plug. It features metal oxide varistor technology, an EMI/RFI noise filter, an LED status indicator and impact resistant casing. It comes with a limited lifetime warranty and a connected equipment guarantee.
