When you hear the name Sylvania immediately you think of quality built, affordable electronics. Well the folks at Sylvania are at it again! introducing the all New SRC100, AM/FM pocket radio. Sometimes it is nice to just fire up the AM radio and take in a ball game. Or grab a lightweight portable FM radio and clip it to your belt and head out for yard work or a nature walk. Sylvania has built a perfect radio for all types of situations. From keeping track of weather when the power is out or having your favorite station on while you are out in the garage working on projects. The powerful speaker sounds great and Sylvania has provided a 3.5mm input jack if you wanted to listen to another source. It's ran by 2x AA batteries (not included) and comes with a very convenient belt clip. This is all from the makers at Sylvania, the leader in AM/FM pocket radios.
