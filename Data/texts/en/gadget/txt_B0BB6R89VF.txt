The Belkin 6-Outlet Surge Protector Power Cube protects your electronic devices from power surges and electrical spikes, so you can charge as many devices as you need to without worrying about a short-circuit. The Belkin 6-Outlet Power Cube measures just under 3 inches all around and comes with an 5-foot cable, allowing you to charge conveniently from anywhere without worrying about workspace clutter.
