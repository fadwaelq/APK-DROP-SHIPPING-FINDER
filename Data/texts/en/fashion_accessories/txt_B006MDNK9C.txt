Somewhere between a high-brow oxford and a sporty sneaker is Skechers's Alley Cat Utility shoe. This sedate offering in scuff-resistant leather boasts a padded collar for extra cush and a rubber lug sole for the grip you need to do what you got to do in Skechers. Skechers is an award-winning global leader in the lifestyle footwear industry that designs, develops, and markets lifestyle footwear that appeals to trend-savvy men, women and children. The company's success stems from it's high quality, diversified, and affordable product line that meets consumers' various lifestyle needs. Since it's inception in 1992, the Skechers diverse product offering has grown from utility style boots to include seven Skechers brands and five uniquely branded fashion lines for men and women.
