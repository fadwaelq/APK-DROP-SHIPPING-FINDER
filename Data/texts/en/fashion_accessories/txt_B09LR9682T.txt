Whether you're exploring your city, running errands, heading out for a stroll or spending long days on your feet, the New Balance 496v3 Cush+ walking shoe has you – and your feet – covered. These walking shoes are stacked with technologies to help keep you comfortable throughout the day, including a Cush+ midsole that delivers ultra-soft, all-day comfort without sacrificing support, and an NB Comfort Insert that provides an exceptionally plush feel underfoot. The outsole features innovative Walking Strike Path technology for added stability and to guide your foot through the walking gait cycle to deliver added comfort, support and sure-footing with every step. Finished with a leather and mesh upper that includes flexible overlays, these comfortable walking shoes have a lightweight fit and feel to help get you through all-day wear with ease. Plus, the classic running shoe profile and design details create a streetwear-inspired look, so you can style them with anything in your closet.
