For effortless, feminine style and plenty of cushiony comfort, slip on a pair of Sanuk Women’s Yoga Sling 2 sandals. These casual, sling sandals have soft, stretchy, fabric straps to ensure a secure fit and stretch with movement. To put on, gently pull the heel strap away from your toes as you slide your foot onto the footbed like a flip flop. The strap fits comfortably between your toes and around your heel. Made from real yoga mat material, the squishy, soft footbeds provide cushioning and comfort for your feet. Durable, flexible outsoles made of partially recycled EVA and straw are one of Sanuk’s sustainability initiates of using recycled materials in their footwear. Because they are flexible, lightweight, and stylishly versatile, Yoga Sling 2 sandals are easily packable and a great choice for taking along on vacation. These Sanuk sandals are machine washable, but do not put them in dryer or expose them to prolonged periods in direct sunlight. A little air drying after getting washed or wet is all the Yoga Sling 2 needs to look their best.
