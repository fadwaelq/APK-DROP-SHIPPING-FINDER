The Second Shift Steel Toe work boot delivers protection, comfort and durability to get you through your day regardless of the work at hand. Rugged styling and quality leathers make this boot an easy everyday choice on and off the job. As the world's foremost manufacturer of heavy equipment, Cat earthmovers are known around the world as a symbol of honest work, strength and integrity. Cat Footwear makes boots and shoes based on these same principals. The brand has evolved from work boots into a range of industrial and casual footwear built with the sole purpose of staying true to their original goal--creating genuine, hard-working boots and shoes.
