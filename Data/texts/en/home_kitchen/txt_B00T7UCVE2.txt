The complex collection of rugs is created by tightly weaving super soft chenille fibers into a versatile flat weave. Crafted by hand, artisans weave chenille yarn through a cotton warp to create a tighter weave to ensure that each rug will lay flat and wear well. This flat-weave area rug is finished with soft white cotton fringe and is completely reversible. With bright aqua tones blended into white, this rug will provide an excellent decorating piece that would be suitable for casual as well as formal settings. This rug is a versatile, stylish, and environmentally conscientious choice as it is made from 100% natural fibers.
