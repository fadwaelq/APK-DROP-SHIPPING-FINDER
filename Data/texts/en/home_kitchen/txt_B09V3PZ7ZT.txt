Our Carnival collection is like its name, unique and full of personality. It’s a sustainable rug made from “recycled/repurposed” yarn that otherwise would end up in a landfill. Since these rugs are environmentally friendly, and made from an array of yarns, no two rugs will be identical, but we keep these exceptional rugs to the same color families (earth tone or multi-color). So, let’s have some fun and enjoy the Carnival, by Garland Rug.
