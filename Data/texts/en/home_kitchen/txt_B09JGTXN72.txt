Product Description This fabric cover photo album features a frame to insert a favorite photo and a deluxe rounded bookbound spine. The album's patented Bi-Directional pockets hold horizontal or vertical photos up to 4 x 6 inches. There is a memo writing area next to each pocket. Photos are displayed two per page. The album holds 300 photos up to 4 x 6 inches in optically clear pockets with a shaded paper background. Archival, photo safe: acid, lignin and PVC free.
