The USA Pan non-stick bakeable Jelly Roll cooling rack is perfect for cooling freshly baked cookies and savory goods, like roasted vegetables, straight from the oven. The USA Pan non-stick Jelly Roll cooling rack fits seamlessly inside the USA Pan Jelly Roll pan and provides home bakers with an easy way to add the perfect glaze or finish to your homemade baked goods. This cooling rack features a steel cross wire design and raised feet, which elevates your baked goods into a raised position that serves to promote Uniform cooling among your baked items. The non-stick coating on the rack surface also means that your baked goods with be easily removable and ready to serve. The cooling rack is oven safe up to 450 degrees Fahrenheit. The cooling rack is not dishwasher safe and it is recommended that you clean the rack using a soft sponge or kitchen brush to remove food particles.
