From Perfectly Seared Meats To Light, Fluffy Omelets, Briolette's Ceramic Nonstick Coating Cooks Food Beautifully. This Material Resists Scratches, Abrasions And Offers A Smooth, Compact And Uniform Surface That Makes It Easier To Clean And Is Stain Resistant. The Ceramic Coating Is Applied At A Low Temperature, Reducing Co2 Emissions And Is Poi-Free. Briolette's Aeternum Cookware Is Suitable For Use On Gas, Electric, Glass And Ceramic Stoves.
