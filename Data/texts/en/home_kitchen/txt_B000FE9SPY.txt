Easily heat, steep, and serve hot tea using this convenient countertop appliance. Accommodating bagged and loose tea, the all-in-one tea maker brews up to 28 ounces of hot tea with variable steeping control to serve individual tastes. The unit offers a simple control knob for adjusting the settings, a front-access "on/off" switch, and a tea basket that is removable for convenient filling and cleaning. The hot-tea maker's keep-warm function ensures the last cup is as hot and flavorful as the first. In addition, the unit's stylish glass teapot with measuring marks and brushed chrome accents provides a generous handle and drip-free spout for graceful serving. An attractive addition to any kitchen counter, the 850-watt hot-tea maker measures approximately 8-3/4 by 9 by 10-1/2 inches and carries a one-year warranty.
