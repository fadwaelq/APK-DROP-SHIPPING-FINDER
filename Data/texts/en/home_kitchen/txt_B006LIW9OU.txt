The OXO Good Grips Folding Sweater Dryer features a generously sized mesh drying surface to lay out delicate clothes for fast, easy drying. Clothes can aerate without any risk of stretching, losing shape, leaving imprints or creasing, making it ideal for items like sweaters and blouses. The legs of the Dryer are offset from the corners to provide maximum drying space while still being able to rest on smaller surfaces such as countertops, washing machines or dryers. When not in use, the legs fold in and the Dryer folds in half for flat, compact storage. Purchase multiple Sweater Dryers for sturdy and efficient stacking. The Dryers fit together securely for maximum stability and the long legs leave ample space between layers for aeration as well as easy loading and unloading.
