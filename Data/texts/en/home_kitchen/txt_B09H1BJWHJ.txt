The LUCID All-Season Microfiber Comforter is weighted just right to keep you sleeping comfortably during the winter and summer months. This hypoallergic comforter uses down-alternative microfiber to provide a plush and cloud-like experience without any of the allergens that easily collect in traditional down comforters. A box-stitched design helps maintain even fill distribution throughout the comforter, so you get the same premium comfort top to bottom. This product is machine washable and dryer safe to help maintain a fresh sleep environment.
