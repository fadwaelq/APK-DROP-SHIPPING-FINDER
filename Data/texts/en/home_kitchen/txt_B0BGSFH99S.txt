The Commercial Chef small countertop microwave provides a compact footprint while boasting up to 700 watts of power. With 0.7 cubic feet of interior space, this kitchen microwave is perfect for preparing snacks, heating up leftovers and defrosting for food preparation. Add even more cooking variety with 6 one-touch quick cook menu buttons: Popcorn, Potato, Pizza, Beverage, Reheat & Frozen Food. Recessed door handle with child safety lock provides a flat-front surface that is easy to open and stays closed. Push button controls with LED display also include a digital clock and timer for easy & simple operation. Start/stop push button helps begin and quickly shuts off the microwave when you need it. The 9.5" glass turntable is removable and dishwasher safe for easy cleaning. Turntable automatically rotates contents to provide even cooking throughout and to prevent one side cooking quicker or slower than the other. Defrost by weight according to the food you need, or use the Speed Defrost feature. This setting works by alternating between defrost and stand times to quickly and effectively defrost foods. Compact microwave measures 17.75" W x 13.13" D x 10.25" H. UL listed.
