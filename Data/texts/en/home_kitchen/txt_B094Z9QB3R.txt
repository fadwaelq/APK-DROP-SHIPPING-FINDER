When you need a place for refuse and space is at a premium, this round stainless steel, step trash can is a lifesaver. Compact in design, this bin fits well in bathrooms, bedrooms, offices or wherever room is limited. The stainless steel finish is fingerprint resistant which prevents smudges to keep the can looking shiny and new. Hygienic in use, the foot pedal allows you to keep your hands clean and sanitized which is important in stopping the spread of germs. The soft close lid keeps the lid from slamming down for a silent close. The lid closes firmly to keep pets out of trash helping you avoid messes and prevents them from bringing you embarrassing gifts when you have visitors. Use this receptacle as is next to your desk for paper waste or add a bag liner for messier locations. The removable bucket makes it a breeze to remove your trash. Best of all, there is no assembly required. Simply remove the can from the box and place it wherever you wish.
