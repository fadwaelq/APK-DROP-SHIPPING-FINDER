The BINO produce saver container offers a clever solution to keep your fruits and vegetables fresh and crisp. It is made of durable, BPA-free plastic and features an adjustable air vent, a removable drainer basket, and an airtight lid. With this container, you can adjust the air ventilation to allow just enough airflow to keep the produce at the right humidity level, while the removable draining basket helps to catch any extra moisture or condensation. The airtight lid locks in the freshness and seals out the outside air to prevent spoiling. This container is great for storing all types of fruits and vegetables, as it comes in a variety of sizes to accommodate all types of produce. It is freezer-safe and easy to clean. This produce saver can also serve as a fridge organizer and pantry organizer.
