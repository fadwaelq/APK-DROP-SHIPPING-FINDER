Product Description The simplehuman in-cabinet bin hangs inside your kitchen cabinet door for quick and easy access while you are busy at the kitchen counter. The heavy-duty steel frame hooks securely onto the door or can be screwed in for a permanent mount. It also holds a durable plastic bucket that keeps your cabinet free from messy spills and lifts out for easy cleaning. Although the bucket is carrier bag compatible, the simplehuman code R custom fit liner fits this bin perfectly – no bunching or slipping for a cleaner experience. Brand Story We design tools that help people become more efficient at home. Day to day life has room for improvement. That's the basic thinking behind simplehuman. Many activities in our daily routine are so basic that we take them for granted. Washing our hands, cleaning up after a meal, grooming and prepping our appearance. Through innovation and new technologies, we find ways to streamline these simple tasks. We call our products tools for efficient living.
