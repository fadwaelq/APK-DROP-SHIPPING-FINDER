The cheese vault by Capabunga is the ideal solution for storing your artisan cheeses. Cheese experts agree that storing cheese in plastic wrap is the Worst possible solution because cheese needs to breathe, reducing excess moisture to prevent Molding. Until now, the only other solutions for cheese storage were disposable paper, waxed cloth, tin foil or plastic bags – all of them end up as landfill. It is brilliantly simple to use – just put the cheese inside, put the lid on top, and store in the refrigerator the cheese vault is made of food-grade, BPA free silicone and the innovative design wicks away excess moisture which extends freshness and reduces mold. It comes with a removable divider; when inserted, easily store two smaller pieces of cheese, keeping each piece separated, fresh and tasty without co-mingling flavors. The lid has a snug fit, so odors won’t get in or out. For added convenience, use a ball point pen to write the cheese name and date on the polished end of the container and wipe it off when needed. The cheese vault measures 6 L x 4 W x 3.5 H. It is completely dishwasher safe and 100% re-usable; we suggest scrubbing out the corners to be sure all crumbs are removed.
