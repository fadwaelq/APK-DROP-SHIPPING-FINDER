This light walnut coffee table offers modern minimalist design, chic style and practical storage, all in one smart package. This coffee table has a tabletop and lower shelf divided into three cube storage cubbies. With the cubbies, you can keep items on display or stowed away for easy access. Each cube measures 12.75" H x 13.125" W x 13.8" D, about the same size as most standard cube organizer cubbies, so they fit bins or baskets designed for use with most cube shelves. Use bins or baskets with the cubes to tuck clutter away and keep items organized neatly, or display items out in the open inside each cubby. Stack magazines or coffee table books in an open cubby and use a bin in another cubby to store away remotes, gaming console controllers and other small items that can contribute to a cluttered appearance. This table is the perfect height to use as a coffee table with most other furniture, but with its functional design and smart, slight profile, it can be used almost anywhere in the home. Use it as a media console or storage shelf under a window or at the foot of a bed. Because the cubes are the perfect size for vinyl records, the table is perfect to store and display records while also housing a record player on top. This modern coffee table is made of high quality engineered wood covered in a smooth and durable faux light woodgrain laminate finish. The engineered wood is durable while still being lightweight, and the laminate surface makes it easy to wipe clean with a damp cloth. The table’s frame is made of painted black steel for added durability and a clean look. This piece is easy to put together, comes ready to assemble and coordinates with other items in the Household Essentials Jamestown Mid-Century Walnut collection.
