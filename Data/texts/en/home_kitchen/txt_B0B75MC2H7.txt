Crack open a cold one, and keep it cold with the new Simple Modern Ranger Can Coolers. We took our insulated stainless steel bottle and tumbler technology and created can holders in two sizes that’ll keep canned drinks icy. Just press your favorite drink into the Ranger Can Cooler, pop it open and start sipping! A silicone ring at the opening of the Ranger will keep your drink securely in place. The Simple Modern Ranger Can Coolers keep annoying condensation on your can or bottle at bay and eliminate the need for coasters. Plus, they’ll keep your hands and fingertips from getting cold as you drink. Available in two sizes: Regular and Slim. The Regular fits standard 12oz aluminum cans, and the Slim is perfect for 12oz skinny cans. These can coolers do not fit bottles.
