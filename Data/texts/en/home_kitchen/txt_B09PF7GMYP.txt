Product Description simplehuman's wall mount grocery bag dispenser mounts to the wall or inside a cabinet, storing bags for reuse. Easily tuck bags through the top opening for clutter-free storage. When ready to reuse, just pull one out of the wide dispensing slot in the front. Easy to install with screws or double-sided tape (both included). Through innovation and new technologies, we find ways to streamline these simple tasks. We call our products tools for efficient living.
