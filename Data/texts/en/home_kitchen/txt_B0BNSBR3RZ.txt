Minimize microwave messes with Tovolo’s Collapsible Microwave Food Cover. Its pop-up design allows you to cover dishes up to 3" tall to eliminate messy splatters then collapse it to a slim 0.75" for easy storage. The vented cover contains splatters without trapping steam, which eliminates both messes and troublesome condensation. The airflow encourages even heating for a perfectly cooked meal. With the help of the easy grip handle, you can remove the cover from your food quickly. Sturdy plastic and silicone are BPA-free, food-safe, and dishwasher-safe.
