Calyx Interiors 1" Cordless Vinyl Mini Blind blinds are clean, practical and easy to install. This white blind will provide a crisp, modern look to any room. The vinyl slat composition will perform well in rooms that have some moisture like the kitchen or bathroom. Calyx Interior Mini Blinds can be easily cared-for, simply dust or use soap and water and your blinds will work well and look as good as new for years. If you plan to mount on the inside, please ensure your window-recess is at least 1/4 inches larger than the blind size that you purchase. Also, make certain that you have a 3/4" inches of depth/flat-space to install the mounting brackets or 2" if you want your blinds mounted flush with your frame.
