Safely and easily open your canned goods with the GoodCook Safe Cut Can Opener. This manual can opener features a unique design that effortlessly opens cans without leaving any sharp edges, and the guide wire lifts the lid from the can once cut fully open so you can safely remove it. Simply rotate the turn knob to release the lid from the opener - with no sharp edges, the lid can be reused as a cover for temporary storage. The durable plastic handle and non-slip turn knob provide a secure grip for additional safety. This handheld can opener is designed with a hardened stainless steel cutting blade and thick stainless steel guide wire for durability and long-lasting use, and it is easy to clean.
