Add a touch of style to your home with the Chelsea Standard Decorative Window Curtain Rod by Kenney. The 5/8 inch diameter Chelsea curtain rod in brushed nickel features matching decorative ball finial ends. Use in your kitchen, bedroom, dining room, and living room to hang light to medium weight curtains.
