Our pure beeswax taper candles are classic candlesticks dipped into pure molten beeswax at just the right temperature by a steady hand and a keen eye. They are dipped and cooled over twenty times each before a pair can be cut free from the rack. Kept out of drafts, these tapers will burn virtually dripless for an hour per inch of length. These taper candles fit standard candle holders — they're 7/8" inches in diameter at the base.
