A perfect alternative to the cost and upkeep on natural wicker, the KOVOT Poly-Wicker Bread Basket is excellent for displaying and serving bread loafs, baguettes, and pastries. Made of durable, food-grade hand woven polypropylene fibers, the baskets are dishwasher and microwave safe and will not mold like natural rattan. Each bread basket measures: 14.5"L x 6.5"W x 3"H .
