First generation version of the BlenderBottle Classic includes a wire whisk that moves freely inside the bottle to mix ingredients. Features a secure screw-on lid, a wide mouth for easy mixing, and embossed markings that measure both ounces and milliliters. The BlenderBottle Classic is dishwasher safe, BPA- and phthalate-free, and comes in two sizes: 20-Ounce (note: measurements only go to 12 ounces), and 28-Ounce (note: measurements only go to 20 ounces).
