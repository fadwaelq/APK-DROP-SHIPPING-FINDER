The sleek KitchenAid 11lb Glass surface kitchen scale features an attractive, stainless steel accent trim and an oversized knob to set units of measurement. This scale will match many kitchen decors. Glass is a naturally stain and odor free surface and is tempered for safety. The large 1. 0" Backlit readout is easy-to-read. An add and weigh Tare function helps to zero out weights of containers before adding food or weighing multiple ingredients together. Conveniently converts both dry and liquid measurements.
