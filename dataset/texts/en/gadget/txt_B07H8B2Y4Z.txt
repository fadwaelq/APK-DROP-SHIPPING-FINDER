Warm tips: 1. This particular power cable does not have the capability to enable the data transfer feature.  2. This is a Mini-B USB Cable, not a Micro Cable, please note that there are many types of USB connectors, make sure that you fully understand what kinds of cable you need, this cable do ONLY work on devices with USB Mini-B port  3. The difference between Mini-B and Micro-B is that the Micro-B is half as thick while the width is the same----Mini-B is 3x7mm / Micro-B is 1.5x7mm
