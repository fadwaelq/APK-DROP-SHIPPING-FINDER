Toshiba presents a top-loading MP3 CD cassette player boom box with a built-in AM/FM radio tuner. Play your favorite music or MP3 CDs from the CD player, cassettes with the tape deck or audio files with the aux input. The telescopic FM antenna provides clearer reception, and the high-output stereo speakers fill your home with dynamic audio. Attractive exterior adds a stylish touch to any room. And the easy-to-read LCD panel lets you locate the CD controls quickly, even in the dark.
