Product Description The Cord Storage Reel is designed to hold up to 150 feet of 16/3 extension cord. The side mounted handle makes it simple to wind up your cord and the wide stable base means the Cord Storage Reel will stay in place without falling down on the job. The convenient carry handle makes the Cord Storage Reel exactly that...convenient to carry to and from the job site. Made from shatter and temperature resistant thermoplastic, the Cord Storage Reel will hold up to the elements year after year. From the Manufacturer Cord manager is a deluxe 150 ft capacity reel cord holder for indoor and outdoor extension cords.
