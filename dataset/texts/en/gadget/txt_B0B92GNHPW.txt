Fujifilm Instant Color Film Instax is an ISO 800 wide picture format integral daylight color film designed for use with Fujifilm instax series cameras. This glossy film yields superb results under both daylight and electronic flash conditions. Its improved picture quality and greater ease of use make it ideal for snapshots and portraits. Furthermore, its easy-to-file size makes it an excellent choice for documentary or archival purposes, as well as a wide variety of other applications.
