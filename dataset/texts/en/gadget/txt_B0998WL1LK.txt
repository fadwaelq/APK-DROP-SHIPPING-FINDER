Elevate your workspace to the next level with the Konnect Monitor Stand. By elevating your monitor to a comfortable eye level, it helps to improve viewing angle and eye stress. Ideal for a laptop or monitor, this monitor stand has four adjustable height options and a bonus storage area underneath to keep small keyboards, notebooks and other desk accessories organized. The built-in cord routing channel helps to easily route any unsightly wires and cables to keep them from getting into a tangled mess on your desk. The non-slip rubber feet ensure that your monitor or laptop will stay secure on the monitor stand.
