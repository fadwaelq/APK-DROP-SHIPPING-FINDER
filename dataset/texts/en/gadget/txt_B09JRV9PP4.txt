The entire Meta Portal family is video calling designed to bring people closer. Simple to set up and fun to use, Meta Portal is a smart video calling device for a closer connection to friends and family—wherever they are. Meta Portal’s simple privacy settings mean you can easily disable the camera and microphone, or block the lens with the camera cover.
