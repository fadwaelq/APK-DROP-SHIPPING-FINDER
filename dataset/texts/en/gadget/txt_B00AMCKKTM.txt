PEX4S232485 PCI Standard Profile Serial Combo Card is a single card solution that is compatible with nearly any serial communication protocol, supporting RS-232, RS-422, RS-485 2-wire or RS-485 4-wire operating modes. This card lets you to add two RS 232 and two RS422/485 serial ports to your PC through a PCI-Express expansion slot. Interface: RS-232, 422, 485 serial. Data transfer rate: Up to 460 Kbps. Ports: 4 x 9-pin DB-9 male RS-232/422/485 serial. Form factor: Plug-in card. Platform supported: PC. Number of ports: 4. Compatibility: Windows 8 (32/64bit), 7 (32/64), Vista(32/64), XP(32/64), 2000, Windows Server 2008 R2, 2003(32/64), Linux Kernel 2.6.x. Card type: Standard profile. Chipset ID: SystemBase- SB16C1052PCI. Connector type: 1 x PCIe male. Operating Temperature: 0 to 100 deg C. Dimensions: 2.7"(H) x 0.6"(W) x 5.3"(L). Standard and low-profile bracket included, supports 2-wire/4-wire RS485 modes, 256-byte deep FIFO per transmitter and receiver. Applications: Monitor and control industrial machines on an assembly line or in a production environment, Bank teller workstations in either full profile or low profile, self-service automated machines and kiosks to control serial devices. Package contents: 1 x PCIe serial combo card, 1 x breakout cable, 1 x low profile bracket, 1 x driver CD, 1 instruction manual. Weight: 2.4 oz. Certification and standards: Green compliant, CE, ROHS, FCC, PCI power management 1.1, PCI-express specification 1.0a. Warranty: Lifetime limited.
