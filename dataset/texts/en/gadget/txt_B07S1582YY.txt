Jabra Move Style Edition is designed for music on the move. These wireless headphones feature the crisp digital sound of Jabra’s signature (Digital Signal Processing) DSP that gives your music, movies and calls true depth and real clarity. The clean, simple, Scandinavian design encompasses a powerhouse of sound and functionality that’s as durable as it is stylish. You can take control of your music and calls directly from these wireless Bluetooth headphones with conveniently placed buttons that allow you to keep your phone in your pocket. Keep the music going all day long. Jabra Move Style Edition has an ultra-light, adjustable headband that stays comfortable, even used over an extended period of time. With up to 14 hours of battery life on a single charge and up to 12 days of standby, these cordless headphones are designed to keep up with your busy day. Plus, a 3.5mm jack connector is included in case you run out of battery or want to listen on an airplane. Jabra Move Style Edition keeps you comfortably taking calls and listening to your music, no matter where you are.
