Description Built for Speed and Reliable Connectivity The Cat7 flat network Ethernet cable is capable of transmitting data at speeds of up to 10Gbps over 100m run of cable. It is made out of 4 shielded twisted pair (STP) of copper wires with two RJ45 connectors on each end. Compared to the Cat 6 network Ethernet cable, the additional shielding and improved quality in twisting of the wires provides better protection from crosstalk, noise, and interference that can degrade the signal quality.
