The VideoSecu articulating mount support most mid size flat panels up to 44 lbs with VESA 75x75/100x100/200x100/200x200mm. This is one of the popular medium size TV mount, with heavy duty steel construction. The two-link arm design provides up to 170 degrees of swivel, 15 degrees tilt up or down, and 2"-14" extension, make it easier for get perfect viewing angle. It also offers a few degrees roll adjustment for level the mount in different positions. Shipped with standard hardware pack.   Will the mount fit my TV?   Firstly, please check the weight of your display, it should be less than 44 lbs. Secondly, If your Monitor or TV has 4 mounting holes on the back of the screen that are 200x200mm/200x100/100x100/75x75 (8"x8"/8"x4"/4"x4"/3"x3") apart from each other vertically and horizontally this bracket will fit. In addition, please check the area inside the 4 mounting holes to make sure there are not any plugs. Otherwise, the plate may block the plugs. Notice: This mount fits most 27-47" TVs (VESA 200x200mm(8x8")/200x100mm(8x4")/100x100mm(4x4")/75x75mm(3x3")), some up to 50 inch TVs manufactured in recent 3 years
