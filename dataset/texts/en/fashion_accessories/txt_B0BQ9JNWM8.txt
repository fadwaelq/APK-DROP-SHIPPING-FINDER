Combining slipper and sandal into a cozy statement shoe, the Fluff Yeah is as good as it sounds and as soft as it looks. Featuring plush sheepskin with a lightweight platform to keep things airy, this standout slide pairs perfectly with midi dresses or your favorite jeans. This product was made in a factory that supports women in our supply chain with the help of HERproject, a collaborative initiative that creates partnerships with brands like ours to empower and educate women in the workplace.
