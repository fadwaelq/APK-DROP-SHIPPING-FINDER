Reebok Men's Classic Renaissance Fashion Sneaker is all about tradition, quality, and clean style. If that is how you roll, these classic walking shoes bring it home. Detailed stitching, a hit of Union Jack flare, and a footbed brilliantly engineered for soft comfort collide for a cosmic performance and the stellar, old-school fashion you crave in your daily footwear. Reebok was founded for one of the best reasons possible: athletes wanted to run faster. So in the 1890s, Joseph William Foster made some of the first known running shoes with spikes in them. By 1895, he was in business making shoes by hand for top runners; and before long his fledgling company, J.W. Foster and Sons, developed an international clientele of distinguished athletes.
