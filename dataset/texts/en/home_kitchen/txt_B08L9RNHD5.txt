If you are looking for chic shelving that will leave your walls feeling light and airy, this floating shelf is the best for the job. Its minimal design cuts-down on frills to leave you with a functional piece that will bring more attention to the items displayed than itself. This shelf is lined with an ashwood woodgrain laminate for a simple yet elegant style. The floating shelf effect is accomplished by the recessed mounting bracket. This allows it to rest flush against the wall. The bracket provides the shelf with the needed support to hold various items. There is 32” of space to display and store items including small house plants, family pictures, memorabilia, decor, books and much more. The simple modern style allows this shelf to blend-in nicely with any surroundings. Mount this in the bathroom to hold lotions and washcloths, the living room to display pictures, the entryway to hold keyrings or the bedroom to house all your favorite books. This shelf stands as a testament to the phrase, “less is more”. The elegant yet minimal style caters to a wide range of needs. The recessed brackets are designed to create a lighter-than-air effect, eliminating any feelings of heaviness..
