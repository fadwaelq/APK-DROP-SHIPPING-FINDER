Ivilon rings comes in a variety of finishes and colors to hang draperies on any type of poles such as cafe rod or window curtain rods of every style. With the Ivilon rings you can hang any panels such as pinch pleats panels, French Drapes and more. Made of iron makes them strong and beautiful. Offered in 3 styles: loop, eyelet or clip Drapery pin hooks required for loop and eyelet rings.
