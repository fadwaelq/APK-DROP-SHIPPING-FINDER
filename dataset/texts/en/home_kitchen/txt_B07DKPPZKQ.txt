The Nifty Solutions 3-tier Cooling Rack helps save valuable countertop room and time, enabling you to cool three racks of baked goodies at the same time. The wire mesh design allows you to cool even your tiniest treats. The 3-tier Cooling Rack includes an additional support arm designed for weightier goodies like cakes and pies. The rack folds up flat for convenient storage.
