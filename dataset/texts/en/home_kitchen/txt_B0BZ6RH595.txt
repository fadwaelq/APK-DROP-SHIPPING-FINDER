Rapidly chill fresh HOT coffee in a minute without dilution from ice cubes! Imagine brewing a cup of hot coffee and being able to quickly turn it into delicious fresh ICED coffee in 60 seconds? The new stainless steel exterior design and stainless-steel interior chilling chambers have the same fast chilling time & zero dilution. The Hyperchiller’s patented design contains two frozen chambers filled with tap water that you freeze inside your freezer until it’s ready to be used. Then you simply brew directly into top of the HyperChiller or pour up to 12.5 ounces of hot coffee, tea or water into the top. The hot beverage is surrounded by ice between the two frozen sections. Swirl the Hyperchiller to chill the hot coffee, and that’s it! In just 60 seconds, your hot coffee is ready to enjoy. Pour it into a serving glass with or without ice, add your fovorite creamer & voila, you have instant gratification! Think about all the time and money you will be saving! It’s fast and simple to use and can chill up to 25 ounces per freeze. The Hyperchiller is compatible with most brewing methods, some even allow it to fit directly beneath a coffeemaker to brew and dispense directly into the HyperChiller. It’s not all about coffee either! You can chill wine, spirits, tea or juice. Plus, cleanup is a cinch. All it needs is a simple rinse or you can place all of the pieces in the top rack of the dishwasher.
