The Incense Match: Lights like a match and burns like incense, available in 16 exciting fragrances. Perfect to throw in a pocket or purse to take with you on the go or leave them in your bathroom to eliminate harsh odors. This lot contains a random assorted pack of 10 unique scents. Possible fragrances include: Bayberry, Coconut, French Vanilla, Hollyberry, Musk, Patchouli, Rain, Spice, Cinnamon, Frankincense, Goddess of Egypt, Jasmine, Oriental Blossom, Potpourri, Sandalwood & Strawberry. When you receive your incense matches, light it just like any normal match, allow it to burn half way then extinguish it and place it in a fire safe dish while it releases it's scent.
